FarmOptic: Precision Crop Guidance

FarmOptic, a system that provides precision crop guidance using a combination of sensors and the node mcu. FarmOptic helps farmers optimize their crops growth by monitoring various environmental factors such as soil pH, moisture levels, temperature, and humidity. With this data, farmers can make informed decisions about irrigation, fertilization, and pest control, ultimately increasing crop yields and reducing resource waste.It also suggests suitable crops for certain environment conditions.
